Perspective: Perils of the Void

To escape this nebula you must change your perspective.

Perspective: Perils of the Void is a reimagining of the original Perspective in which players are tasked with helping intrepid explorer, Vanessa Von Cactus, escape from the perils of deep space.

After Vanessa tests her experimental new spaceship - The Seeker - she winds up stranded in a nebula with no idea how she got there, or how to get out. With the help of her ship’s onboard AI they create a 3D display to pilot their way through the dense gas, asteroids, and other mysteries of space, but the deeper they get the more they worry they won’t like what they find at the center.
