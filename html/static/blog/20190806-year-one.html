Year One

Happy First Birthday to Aletheia Ware LLC.

Today marks one year since Aletheia Ware LLC was founded and this post looks back at the progress made, milestones reached, and achievements earned.

Products & Services

Over the past year Aletheia Ware has built tools to help people and games to entertain them, open sourcing 33 projects containing over 40K lines of code, tests, and documentation.

  • BC - a general purpose blockchain defined in Protocol Buffers and implemented in Go and Java.
  • Alias - an identity database built on BC.
  • Finance - a payment, registration, and subscription database built on BC.
  • Space - a secure, private, and immutable storage platform built on BC.
    • Dashboard Camera - an in-car dashboard camera which records to Space.
    • Surveillance Camera - a motion-activated in-home surveillance camera which records to Space.
  • Joy - a 3D game engine.
  • Perspective - a casual 3D puzzle game built on Joy.

Website

  • Purchased aletheiaware.com and a static IP address.
  • Setup a Raspberry Pi 3B+ with Apache Webserver and a certificate from Let's Encrypt.
  • Wrote HTML, CSS, and SVG.

Legal

  • Retained Legal Council with Borders Law Group.
  • Registered as a Single-Member Limited Liability Company in California.
  • Developed Terms of Service Agreement.
  • Developed Privacy Policy.
  • Developed Beta Test Agreements.
  • Retained CPA with GillinghamCPA.
  • First state and federal taxes filed.

Operational

Registered with several third parties to organize, manage and operate various parts the business;

  • Hover to register domain.
  • Github to host open source code.
  • Digital Ocean to provide hardware infrastructure.
  • Stripe to handle customer billing information and process payments.
  • Chase to hold business finances.
  • Intuit Quickbooks to track and manage finances.
  • Google GSuite to manage communications and collaborations.
  • Google Play Store to publish and distribute apps and games.
