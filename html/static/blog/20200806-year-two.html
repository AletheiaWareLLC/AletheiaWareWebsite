Year Two

Happy Second Birthday to Aletheia Ware LLC.

Today marks two years since Aletheia Ware LLC was founded and this post looks back at the progress made, milestones reached, and achievements earned.

Products & Services

Over the past year Aletheia Ware has continued to build tools to assist people and games to entertain them.

  • Convey - a secure, public, communication platform.
  • Crypto - a general purpose cryptography library.
  • LAB - a collaborative, cross-platform development environment.
  • Perspective: Perils of the Void - an outer space puzzle game.
