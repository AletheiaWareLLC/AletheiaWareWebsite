Mining-as-a-Service

Mining-as-a-Service allows customers to contribute to the blockchain without using their CPU cycles and draining their battery.

Mining is CPU intensive and uses a lot of energy. Mining on mobile devices drains battery and takes longer due to the CPUs running at a lower clock rate than desktop/server equivalent.

Aletheia Ware provides Mining-as-a-Service on a metered rate of $0.01 per group of 1,000,000 bytes (1MB) per month up to a maximum of 2,000,000,000 bytes (2GB). The table below gives examples of the cost for different monthly usages.
